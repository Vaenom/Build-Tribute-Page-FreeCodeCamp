Every House Has A Superhero

A biopic about my father

This generation and movie makers have vastly gloried the term "Superhero". We see them as all-mighty with powers that science cannot explain. But, the truth is- not every hero wears cape. Anyone and everyone can become a superhero by contributing for the betterment. And, my father is a superhero too.

Background Information

He was born on 8th of July, 1969 A.D. at Lamatar-08, Lalitpur, Nepal.

He has educational qualification upto only 10th standard and could not complete high school because of family restraints- death of his father.

Married at the age of 13, he has 2 sons and 2 daughters. His wife is a housewife. Elder daughter is a doctor and the younger one is a company manager. His older runs his own textile company and the younger one is I.T. student with freelancing as a side gig.

Career Highlights and Contributions

He started teaching at the age of 19 at the government school near his locality as a primary teacher until he got transferred to another school which was 18 km away. He used to walk all the way as there was no means of transport available during his period until he finally retired at the age of 60.

He is the founder of a non-profit organization called "Helping Hands" which primarily focuses on providing study materials to the children of rural areas and those who are financially weak.

He is also the founder and weekly contributor at "Community Hub". This is a non-profit organization that has libraries for the ones who seek knowledge. It also teaches music to the interested free of cost. It runs on donation of the community and is handled by community.

He is a leader of "Change Starts from Us" campaign whose main focus is to aware the communities about how to make their own community cleaner safer and hygienic.

"Nothing is given to man on earth - struggle is built into the nature of life, and conflict is possible - the hero is the man who lets no obstacle prevent him from pursuing the values he has chosen."

-- A quote from father's diary
